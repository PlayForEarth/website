Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2025  ·  Play for Earth

1. Introduction

Play for Earth ("we", "us", "our") is committed to protecting the privacy of individuals who use our platform and visit our website. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal data.

2. Data We Collect

We collect the following types of data:

  • Account data: name, email address, organisation, role
  • Verification data: photos, geo-location timestamps, action logs submitted through the platform
  • Usage data: platform interactions, session data, device and browser type
  • Communication data: emails and messages sent to us

3. How We Use Your Data

We use collected data to:

  • Provide and operate the Play for Earth platform
  • Verify field actions and generate ESG/EPR compliance evidence
  • Generate analytics dashboards for client organisations
  • Communicate with users about their accounts and our services
  • Improve platform performance and user experience
  • Comply with legal obligations

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell personal data. We may share data with:

  • Client organisations: Verified action data is accessible to the relevant client account that deployed the field programme
  • Service providers: Infrastructure partners (hosting, database) who process data on our behalf under strict confidentiality agreements
  • Legal authorities: Where required by applicable law

Data is never shared between separate client accounts.

5. Data Storage and Security

Data is stored in isolated, multi-tenant environments. We implement appropriate technical and organisational security measures including encrypted connections (HTTPS/TLS), access controls, and regular security reviews. No system is 100% secure; we will notify affected users of any significant breach.

6. Data Retention

We retain account and verification data for the duration of the client agreement plus up to 24 months thereafter for audit purposes, unless a shorter retention period is required by applicable law or agreed in a data processing agreement.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Object to or restrict certain processing
  • Data portability

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at play.for.earth.info@gmail.com.

8. Cookies

Our website uses essential cookies for session management and security. We do not use advertising or third-party tracking cookies. You can control cookie settings through your browser.

9. Children's Privacy

Our Services are not directed at children under 18. We do not knowingly collect data from minors. If you believe a minor has provided us with personal data, contact us to have it removed.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Material changes will be communicated to active users by email. Continued use of the Services following notification constitutes acceptance.

11. Contact

For privacy-related enquiries or to exercise your rights, contact us at play.for.earth.info@gmail.com.
