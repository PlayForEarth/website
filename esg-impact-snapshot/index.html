How the snapshot works

Three steps. One week. A clear picture of what's possible in your territory.

01

You fill in the form

Tell us your sector, geography, and what challenge you're trying to solve. Takes under 3 minutes.

02

We map your context

We assess your existing field infrastructure against our verification logic and identify the highest-value use case for your situation.

03

You receive your snapshot

A concise document: what actions you can verify, what proof you'd generate, estimated timeline, and a clear feasibility verdict.
