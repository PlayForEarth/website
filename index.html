The Gap We Close

FMCG brands are flying blind on circularity

EPR regulations are tightening globally. Scope 3 reporting is mandatory. But recovery data from the last mile simply doesn't exist — until now.

📦

No visibility below the distributor

FMCG brands have zero verified data on what happens to their packaging once it leaves the warehouse. Field agents visit thousands of retailers — but none of it is captured or structured.

📋

EPR compliance is built on estimates

Extended Producer Responsibility fees are calculated on declared production — not verified recovery. Brands overpay. Regulators can't enforce. And the circular economy stays theoretical.

📉

Behavior change doesn't stick

Top-down circular commitments rarely reach frontline sales reps or retailers. Without behavioral loops, incentives, or feedback — declared goals stay declared goals.

🏗️

New infrastructure is too expensive

Building parallel collection networks is slow and costly. The people, the routes, and the touchpoints already exist — but there's no system to activate and measure them.

What We Build

The behavioral layer that was always missing

We don't build new infrastructure. We activate the infrastructure that already exists — existing sales reps, existing routes, existing retail relationships — and turn routine visits into structured, verified circular data.

📍

Geo-validated field actions

Every action is anchored to a GPS-verified location. No ghost visits, no fabricated compliance. Territory coverage maps build in real time.

📸

Photo verification at the point of action

Sales reps photograph the collection basket — material type, fill level, condition. Timestamped and geo-tagged. Thirty seconds. Fully auditable.

🔁

Points, streaks, and the Lucky Spin

Every verified action earns points. Streaks multiply them. Hit a milestone and the Lucky Spin unlocks — real rewards, layered onto the visits reps are already making.

🤖

AI-powered territory intelligence

Every field action feeds an analytics layer that generates territory dashboards, recovery performance scores, category benchmarks, and EPR audit-ready reports.
