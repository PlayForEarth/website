The Measurement Standard

GTI: we measure what actually happens — not what brands say.

The Green Transformation Index tells you whether circular behavior is genuinely embedded — or just happening under pressure.

GTI = (F × 0.25) + (S × 0.20) + (A × 0.25)
+ (N × 0.15) + (D × 0.15)

SSpread across territory20%

Transformation Stages
0
Inactive
No circular actions logged. Reps visit outlets but nothing is verified or recorded. The program exists on paper only.
1
Declared
The brand has committed to a recovery program. Collection points are being set up. But field actions are still inconsistent — compliance happens only when someone is watching.
2
Reinforced
Reps are logging verified actions regularly. Incentive loops are working. Behavior is consistent across most of the territory — but still driven by points and reminders, not habit.
Current proof
3
Embedded
Circular actions happen as part of the normal route — without prompting. Reps do it automatically. Retailers expect it. The behavior survives even when incentives are paused.
Roadmap
4
Internalized
Circular behavior is self-sustaining. Reps onboard new colleagues into the habit. Retailers ask why the basket isn’t there if a visit is missed. The system runs itself.
In plain English

We measure frequency (how often) · spread (how many outlets) · persistence (what happens when incentives are removed). The GTI satisfies EPR auditors, Scope 3 frameworks, and DFI impact requirements — simultaneously.
