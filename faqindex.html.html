Questions about Play for Earth

Everything you need to know about how the platform works, who it's for, and how to get started.

The Platform
Play for Earth is a circular economy operating system that activates existing FMCG field agents and sales reps to verify real-world ESG and EPR actions — photo by photo, visit by visit. No new headcount, no new infrastructure required.
We verify actions like device or packaging returns, reuse and refill actions, cold-chain compliance (fridge temperatures), on-site ESG practices, and EPR-related collection activities. Each action is verified via photo, geo-tag, and behavioral data.
Neither. Play for Earth is an execution and verification layer. It doesn't replace your ESG reporting software or external audits — it provides the verified field evidence those systems need. Think of it as the missing proof layer between commitment and report.
No. The platform works entirely through smartphones already carried by field agents. No sensors, no IoT devices, no new equipment purchases. This is a core design principle: we work with what already exists in the field.
Who It's For
Primarily FMCG companies, bottlers, and distributors who need verified EPR compliance and Scope 3 emissions data. Also NGOs, foundations, and development programmes that need field execution infrastructure for circular economy or sustainability programmes.
We're active in Vietnam (20,000+ verified field actions) and developing markets in Africa — Kenya and Zambia are primary targets. We're also tracking EPR regulatory rollouts in Malaysia and Thailand as entry signals.
Yes. While our primary commercial focus is FMCG companies, the platform is designed for any organisation running distributed field programmes that require verified action data — NGOs, public health programmes, and development funders included.
Getting Started
It's a scoped feasibility assessment for your specific context: one use case, one geography, clear output. We map your existing field infrastructure against the platform's verification logic and deliver a concrete picture of what proof you could generate and how fast. No commitment required.
A standard territory onboarding takes 4–6 weeks. This includes field agent configuration, verification logic setup, dashboard configuration, and a pilot run. We offer a one-time onboarding fee per territory (USD 15–35K depending on scope).
Licensing is seat-based at USD 25–60 per user per month, plus a one-time territory onboarding fee. See our Pricing page for details, or contact us for a custom quote.
The best first step is requesting a free ESG snapshot — it's a no-commitment scoping session that gives you a clear picture of what's possible in your context. You can also book a 30-minute demo directly via Calendly. Both options are available on our Contact page.
Legal & Data
Each client environment is isolated in a multi-tenant architecture. Field verification data (photos, geo-tags, timestamps) is stored securely and only accessible to the relevant client account. We do not share data between clients.
