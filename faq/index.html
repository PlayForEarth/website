The Platform
Play for Earth is a circular economy operating system that activates existing FMCG field agents and sales reps to verify real-world ESG and EPR actions — photo by photo, visit by visit. No new headcount, no new infrastructure required.
We verify actions like packaging returns, reuse and refill setups, cold-chain compliance, on-site ESG practices, and EPR-related collection activities. Each action is verified via timestamped photo, GPS geo-tag, and behavioral data.
Neither. Play for Earth is an execution and verification layer. It doesn't replace ESG reporting software or external audits — it provides the verified field evidence those systems need. Think of it as the missing proof layer between commitment and report.
No. The platform runs entirely through smartphones already carried by field agents. No sensors, no IoT devices, no new equipment purchases. This is a core design principle: we work with what already exists.
Who It's For
Primarily FMCG companies, bottlers, and distributors who need verified EPR compliance and Scope 3 emissions data. Also NGOs, foundations, and development programmes that need field execution infrastructure for circular economy or sustainability programmes.
We're active in Vietnam (20,000+ verified field actions) and Africa — Kenya is our headquarters market. We're tracking EPR regulatory rollouts in Malaysia, Thailand, and East Africa as entry signals.
Yes. The platform suits any organisation running distributed field programmes that require verified action data — NGOs, public health programmes, and development funders included.
Getting Started
A scoped feasibility assessment: one use case, one geography, clear output. We map your existing field infrastructure against the platform's verification logic and deliver a concrete picture of what proof you could generate — and how fast. No commitment required. Request yours →
A standard territory onboarding takes 4–6 weeks: field agent configuration, verification logic setup, dashboard config, and a pilot run. One-time onboarding fee per territory (USD 15–35K depending on scope).
Seat-based licensing at USD 25–60 per user per month, plus a one-time territory onboarding fee. See full pricing → or contact us for a custom quote.
Legal & Data
Each client runs in an isolated multi-tenant environment. Verification data (photos, geo-tags, timestamps) is stored securely and only accessible to that client. Data is never shared between clients.
